Our Services

Architectural Consultancy

Bringing your dream house to reality is our goal. At the same time our focus is to have something very beautiful, efficient and suit the requirements at the cost that is reasonable for ones budget.

Planning & Designing

With optimum use of space we design something that is functional as well as appealing to eyes. You will see optimum use of natural light and ventilation in our planning.

Interior Designing

Combining our knowledge with aesthetic vision to design solutions that are convenient, functional and at the same time attractive, safe and meet the needs of the people, using the space available, is our objective.

Valuation

We deal with valuation of immovable properties based on market rates for availing finance from financial institutions.

Landscape Designing

We make the outdoor spaces enjoyable, multipurpose by creating a tranquil environment by selecting appropriate trees, plants and vegetation to suit the climatic conditions of the region.

Project Management

Technological advancements and magnitude of project affects the complexity of project. With planning, management, organizing, staffing and directing, we achieve the project objectives in the specified scope, quality, time and cost.
